How Our Image Compressor Works

Our tool uses a smart, client-side lossy compression method to shrink photo files. When you need to compress JPEG online, our algorithm intelligently reduces non-essential data. To compress PNG files, it reduces the color palette while preserving full alpha transparency. This all happens in your browser, making it a fast and secure way to reduce image size without a noticeable drop in quality.
