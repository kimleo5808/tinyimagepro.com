Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 28, 2024

Welcome to TinyImagePro! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Our data collection practices vary depending on the specific tool you use:

  • Image Compressor & JPG to PDF Converter:
    These tools operate entirely within your web browser (client-side). Your image files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers. All processing is done on your local device, ensuring your files remain 100% secure and private.
  • Text Remover Tool:
    To provide the AI-powered text removal functionality, the image you upload and the mask you create by painting on it are sent to a third-party API service (e.g., Clipdrop or a similar provider) for processing. After processing, the result is sent back to you. We do not store these images on our servers. We recommend you review the privacy policy of the third-party provider to understand how they handle data.
  • Automatically Collected Information:
    Like most websites, we may use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect non-personal information about your visit, such as your browser type, the time of your visit, the pages you viewed, and referring websites. This information is used to help us analyze traffic and improve our service.

2. Cookies

Our website uses "cookies" to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files placed on your hard drive for record-keeping purposes.

  • We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits.
  • We also partner with third-party vendors, such as Google AdSense, who use cookies to serve personalized ads on our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the relevant ad settings pages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain our website.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Serve advertisements through our partners, such as Google AdSense.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your information. For tools that run client-side, your data never leaves your device. For tools that require interaction with third-party APIs, we use secure HTTPS protocol for data transmission.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website's main page.
