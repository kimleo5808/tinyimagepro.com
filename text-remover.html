Frequently Asked Questions about Text Removal

How do you remove text from an image without damaging the background?

Our tool uses a sophisticated AI technology called "inpainting." Instead of just blurring or smudging the text, the AI analyzes the surrounding background and intelligently fills in the erased area. This ensures the result looks natural and the original background texture and lighting are preserved.

Can I use this text remover for free?

Yes, TinyImagePro's text remover is free to use for standard-resolution images. We believe powerful editing tools should be accessible to everyone. For very high-resolution files or advanced features, we may offer a Pro version in the future.

Is it better than using a clone stamp or blur tool?

Absolutely. While clone stamps and blur tools can cover text, they often leave obvious, unprofessional-looking marks. A blur is easily noticeable, and a poorly used clone stamp creates repetitive patterns. Our AI text remover provides a superior result by genuinely recreating the background for a clean, seamless finish.

What file formats are supported?

You can upload the most common image formats, including JPG (JPEG) and PNG. After you remove text from the image, you will be able to download the final result as a high-quality PNG file.
